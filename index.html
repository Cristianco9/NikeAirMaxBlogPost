The story of Nike Air Max

The Nike Air Max is a emblematic line of shoes produced by Nike Inc. The first model Nike Air Max 1 was released in 1987. This iconic shoes are identified by their midsoles incorporing flexible urethane pouches filled with pressurized gas, visible from the exterior of the shoes and intended to provide cushioning underfoot.


Mario Franklin Rudy was an areospace engineer, who finished his carrier in the late '60s to pursue creative solutions for other industries. one of his audacious ideas: placing tiny air bags in the soles of athletic shoes to soften impact.


Tinker Hatfield is one of the world's most legendary sneaker designers, and currently Nike's Vice President for Design and Special Projects. He designed the originals Nike Air Max 1 silhouette in the 1987.
